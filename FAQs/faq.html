What are the dates and times for the Festival?

The Filipino Youtht Festival will take place from December 8th-10th. The Festival grounds will be open from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM each day, with various events and activities scheduled throughout.

How much does it cost to attend the festival?

General admission tickets are $15 per day for adults and $10 for children under 12. Seniors and students with a valid ID will recieve a 20% discount.

Is there parking available at the festival?

There will be dedicated parking in the festival venue, parking fee will be $10 per day. Parking will be limited though, so there will be shuttle services and other public transportation that will help attendies reach the festival.

What type of food and drinks will be available?

The festival will have a variety of foods and stalls and vendors offering authentic filipino cuisine, including favorites like adobo, pancit, and halo-halo. Vegetarian and vegan options will be available as well.

Are pets allowed?

For the safety and comfort of all attendee and employees, pets are not allowed at the festival, except for service animals. WE appreiciate your understanding and cooperation.
