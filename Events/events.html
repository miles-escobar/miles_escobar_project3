Activities

Activities

  • Cultural showcase of beliefs, art and lifestyles
  • Cooking demonstrations and workshops of popular Filipino dishes and meals

Activities

  • Filipino music concert starring Beabadoobee and Bruno Mars
  • Art and craft exhibitions
  • Closing ceremony with fireworks
